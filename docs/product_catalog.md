# NatureSip: Product Catalog & Detailed Specifications

This document provides a detailed breakdown of the products manufactured by NatureSip (Hari-Kripa), categorized by type. All products are processed using **Flash Drying Technology**.

## 1. Fruit Powders & Tablets
*Processed with Dry Honey Powder for natural sweetness. No additional sugar.*

### **Mango Powder**
- **Process:** Flash dried from premium ripe mangoes.
- **Form:** Fine powder.
- **Key Nutrients:** Vitamin A, Vitamin C, Potassium.
- **Recommended Use:** Smoothies, desserts, or direct mix with water.

### **Banana Powder**
- **Process:** Made from farm-fresh ripe bananas.
- **Form:** Ultra-fine powder.
- **Key Nutrients:** Magnesium, B6, Fiber.
- **Recommended Use:** Energy drinks, baking, infant food supplement.

### **Guava Powder**
- **Process:** Dried pink/white guava pulp.
- **Form:** Fine powder with high aromatic retention.
- **Key Nutrients:** High Lycopene, Vitamin C.
- **Recommended Use:** Mocktails, jams, digestive boosters.

### **Litchi Powder**
- **Process:** Moisture removed from pure litchi pulp.
- **Form:** Highly soluble powder.
- **Key Nutrients:** Copper, Phosphorus.
- **Recommended Use:** Refreshing summer drinks.

### **Jamun Powder / Tablets**
- **Type:** Available in both powder and tablet form.
- **Key Benefits:** Known for managing blood sugar levels and digestive health.
- **Nutrients:** Iron, Vitamin C.

### **Watermelon Powder**
- **Process:** Low-temp dehydration of watermelon juice/pulp.
- **Key Benefits:** Hydration and Citrulline content.

## 2. Vegetable & Spice Powders
*100% Pure, no additives.*

### **Tomato Powder**
- **Process:** Spray/Flash dried from ripe tomatoes.
- **Concentration:** 1kg powder = approx. 12-14kg fresh tomatoes.
- **Use:** Soups, sauces, seasoning.

### **Red & Green Chilly Powder**
- **Process:** Flash dried to retain vibrant color and capsaicin levels without charring.
- **Purity:** 100% Pure Chilly.

### **Garlic & Onion Powder**
- **Note:** Dehydrated at optimal temperatures to avoid bitterness.
- **Form:** Granular or Fine Powder.

### **Turmeric & Coriander Powder**
- **Type:** High Curcumin content for Turmeric.
- **Process:** Traditional spices processed with modern flash drying to preserve essential oils.

### **Amla (Indian Gooseberry) Tablets**
- **Process:** Compressed flash-dried amla powder.
- **Key Benefits:** Highest natural source of Vitamin C, Immunity booster.

## 3. Specialty Ingredients

### **Dry Honey Powder**
- **Function:** Used as a natural sweetener across the fruit range.
- **Benefit:** Low glycemic index compared to sugar, retains enzymatic properties of honey.

### **Kadhai Gravy Base**
- **Description:** A pre-mixed vegetable powder blend for instant culinary use.
- **Ingredients:** Tomato, Onion, Garlic, and traditional spices.
